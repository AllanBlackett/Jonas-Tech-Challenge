About Us

Founded in 1971, Mystic River Country Club is widely recognized as one of North America’s prestigious private golf and country clubs. The club is a member-owned golf and country club limiting its membership to 250 member families.

Mystic River’s vibrant membership community enjoys a world class 18-hole Jack Nicklaus championship golf course that integrates beautifully with the gentle topography of the area and is framed by aged oak forests.

The stunningly manicured grounds house our historic, elegant 30,000 square foot clubhouse and exquisite dining facilities.

Family Oriented

We pride ourselves in creating an elegant and relaxed private club that also has a warm, friendly, family oriented atmosphere. We offer social activities such as holiday events, themed parties etc that enable our members to meet and mingle.

Our amenities include a full service spa, an Olympic sized swimming pool, 6 har-tru tennis courts and a state-of-the-art fitness center.

Mystic River’s exceptional culinary team and professional management staff pride themselves in impeccable personalized service, attention to detail and flawless presentation and quality.

We offer a family oriented atmosphere with several sports programs and social activities. Our golf course enjoys a reputation as one of the best in the state and annually hosts a US Open Qualifier as well as other member and non-member golf tournaments. The aquatics center includes a diving well, a junior Olympic size lap pool and a children's wading pool. In keeping with our family friendly philosophy, our Kidz Klub offers childcare, children's programs and junior activities. Our Executive Chef Steve Tersigni has created a menu that appeals to families as well as to the more adventurous palette.

Welcome to the Mystic River Golf Club, a place where family is celebrated and cherished!
